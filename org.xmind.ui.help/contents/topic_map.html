New Sheet from Topic is a brand new feature to let your master your big map. Here's how:

  1. Open a file.(Map A)
  2. Select a topic.
  3. There are two ways:
    • Choose "Insert > New Sheet from Topic" on the menu.
    • Use shortcut "Ctrl+Alt+T".
  4. XMind will create a new sheet in current workbook. (Map B)

Note:

  1. Central topic in Map B is just the topic you select in Map A.
  2. This central topic is linked with that selected topic.
